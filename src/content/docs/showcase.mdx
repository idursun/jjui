### Change revset with auto-complete

You can change revset with autocomplete and signature help while typing.

![GIF](/jjui/gifs/jjui_revset.gif)

### Rebase

Rebase revisions interactively in the revision tree.

![GIF](/jjui/gifs/jjui_rebase.gif)

See [Rebase](/jjui/revisions/rebase) for details.

### Squash

Squash revisions into one by pressing `shift+s`.

![GIF](/jjui/gifs/jjui_squash.gif)

### Show revision details

Press `l` to open details for the selected revision.

In details mode you can:

- restore selected files with `r`
- split selected files with `s`
- view diffs with `d`

![GIF](/jjui/gifs/jjui_details.gif)

See [Details](/jjui/details).

### Bookmarks

Move bookmarks to selected revisions.

![GIF](/jjui/gifs/jjui_bookmarks.gif)

### Op Log

Press `o` to open oplog. Press `r` to restore the selected operation.

![GIF](/jjui/gifs/jjui_oplog.gif)

See [Oplog](/jjui/oplog).

### Preview

Press `p` to toggle preview for revisions, files, or operations.

While preview is open:

- `ctrl+n`: scroll down
- `ctrl+p`: scroll up
- `ctrl+d`: half-page down
- `ctrl+u`: half-page up

![GIF](/jjui/gifs/jjui_preview.gif)

See [Preview](/jjui/preview).
