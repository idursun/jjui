---
title: Evolog Operation
description: Inspect and restore evolution entries for selected revisions
---

Evolog opens revision evolution history for selected revisions.

Press `v` in Revisions to open Evolog.

## Core actions

- `r`: restore selected evolog entry
- `d`: open full diff for selected evolog entry
- `enter`: apply selected evolog entry

## Navigation and view

- `j`/`k` or arrows: move
- `p`: toggle preview
- `shift+p`: move preview to bottom

## Exit

- `esc`: cancel/close evolog
- `q`: quit jjui
