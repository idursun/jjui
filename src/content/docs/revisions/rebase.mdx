---
title: Rebase
description: Rebase revisions interactively
---

Rebase lets you move selected revisions relative to a target revision.

![GIF](/jjui/gifs/jjui_rebase.gif)

Press `r` in the Revisions view to open rebase mode.

By default:

- source = `revision`
- target = `destination` (onto)

## Source modes

- `r`: `revision` (only selected revision)
- `shift+b`: `branch` (selected revision's branch)
- `s`: `descendants` (selected revision and descendants)

## Target modes

- `o`: `onto` (destination)
- `a`: `after`
- `b`: `before`
- `i`: `insert`
- `t`: open target picker

## Target picker

Press `t` to open a target picker with autocomplete.

- `up`/`down`: move selection
- `tab` / `shift+tab`: autocomplete
- `enter`: apply
- `alt+enter`: force apply
- `esc`: cancel

## Apply and cancel

- `enter`: apply rebase
- `alt+enter`: force apply
- `esc`: cancel

Other useful keys in rebase mode:

- `f`: ace jump
- `@`: jump to working copy
- `j`/`k`, `pgup`/`pgdown`: navigation
