---

The squash operation combines multiple revisions into a single revision.

To activate squash, press `shift+s` in the Revisions view. The cursor moves to the parent of the selected revisions so you can pick a target.

## Options

- `e`: toggle `--keep-emptied`
- `d`: toggle `--use-destination-message`
- `i`: toggle `--interactive`
- `t`: open target picker

## Workflow

1. Select revisions to squash.
2. Press `shift+s` to activate squash mode.
3. Choose target revision directly, or press `t` to open target picker.
4. Toggle options (`e`, `d`, `i`) as needed.
5. Press `enter` to apply (`alt+enter` to force apply).

## Target picker

The target picker uses the shared `revisions.target_picker` bindings:

- `up`/`down`: move
- `tab` / `shift+tab`: autocomplete
- `enter`: apply
- `alt+enter`: force apply
- `esc`: cancel
