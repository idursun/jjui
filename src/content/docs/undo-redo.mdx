---
title: Undo and Redo
description: Browse and apply undo/redo options
---

<img alt="Undo" src="/jjui/images/undo.png" />

jjui provides dedicated Undo and Redo views.

- Press `u` to open Undo.
- Press `shift+u` to open Redo.

In both views:

- `h`: previous option
- `l`: next option
- `enter`: apply selected option
- `esc`: cancel/close

These views are useful when you want to inspect recent operations before applying undo or redo.
