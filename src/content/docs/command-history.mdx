---
title: Command History
description: "Reuse and manage command history for : and $"
---
jjui records every jj command it runs. Results appear as flash messages and are automatically dismissed after 4 seconds. Press `shift+w` to open the command history and review any past command along with its output.

## Keys

- `j`/`k` or arrows: navigate
- `d`: delete the selected entry
- `shift+w` or `esc`: close
